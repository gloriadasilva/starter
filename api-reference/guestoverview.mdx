---
title: "Guest Session"
description: "Get to know more about guest session"
icon: 'circle-user'

---

Guest session is another type of user authentication. It allows users to perform some functionalities of a TMDB account without creating one.

For example, some of the things users can do with a guest session are maintain a rated list, a watchlist, and a favorite list.

<Warning>
          Guest session_id will automatically be deleted if they are not used

          within 24 hours after being created.
</Warning>
