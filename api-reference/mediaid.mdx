`media_id` is a required parameter for certain endpoints that retrieve movies or TV shows.

Follow these steps to get a `media_id`\`&#x20;

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1">
    Search for the particular movie or TV show you wish to add to your favorites [here](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step 2">
    Get your `media_id` from the URL.
  </Step>
</Steps>
